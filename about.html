Hamaray Maqasid

Hannan Welfare & Development Organization aik ghair-siyasi aur falahi tanzeem hai jo Sahiwal aur us ke gird-o-nawah ke dehai ilaqon ki taraqqi par kaam kar rahi hai. Humara maqsad dehaton ko modern infrastructure aur behtar sahhuliyat se araasta karna hai.

Humara maqsad insaniyat ki khidmat aur har ghar tak bunyadi sahuliyat pahunchana hai.
